2D Camera Demo

The big blue rectangle is the whole world. The white rectangle is the camera. The smaller dark viewport is what the player actually sees on screen.

The core idea

Objects do not really move because of the camera. They stay at their own world position. When drawing, we subtract the camera position.

screenX = worldX - cameraX
screenY = worldY - cameraY

Try this

Move the player with WASD, arrow keys, or the buttons. In the middle of the world, the camera follows. Near the world edges, the camera stops so it does not show empty space outside the map.
